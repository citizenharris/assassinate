🔪 Assassin 🔪

Players

    Rooms

    Toggle the rooms in your house. Active rooms build the weapon pool — weapons and locations are then assigned independently.

    The rules
    1. Kill your victim with the specified weapon, only in the specified location.
    2. You must hand them the weapon — throwing it doesn't count.
    3. The weapon must be handed over undisguised.
    4. A successful assassination means you inherit your victim's target.
    5. Last one standing wins.
    Be creative. The game is afoot!
